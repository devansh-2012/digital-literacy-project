# "Stay Safe Online" Prevention Guide

1. **PIN Protocol:** Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. [cite_start]A PIN is only required for sending or checking your balance.
2. **Verify Identity:** Always verify the name of the person you are transacting with before clicking "Pay" on any app.
3. **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Enable 2FA on your college email and all social media accounts.
4. **Link Caution:** Never click on "KYC update" or "Account Blocked" links received via SMS or WhatsApp.
5. **Strong Passwords:** Use unique, complex passwords for different platforms; avoid using your birthdate or "123456".
6. **Public Wi-Fi:** Avoid logging into bank accounts or making payments while connected to free public Wi-Fi on campus.
7. **App Updates:** Keep your UPI and banking apps updated to ensure you have the latest security patches.
8. **Privacy Settings:** Regularly review your social media privacy settings to limit the amount of personal data visible to strangers.

### 🚨 How to Report:
If you or a peer are victims of cybercrime, report it immediately:
* **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in) 
* **National Helpline No.:** **1930** (Available 24x7)
